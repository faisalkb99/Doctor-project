Appointments Patient

Summary: During the consultation, the patient reported experiencing a persistent headache characterized by a dull ache and occasional pressure around the temples over the past week. They mentioned blurry vision, particularly at times when the headache occurred. Further discussion revealed heightened stress levels at work and irregular sleep patterns. The doctor suggested keeping a headache diary to track symptoms and potential triggers. While advising over-the-counter pain relievers for immediate relief, the doctor also proposed scheduling tests, including an eye exam, to investigate the underlying causes of the headaches and blurred vision. The patient agreed to follow these recommendations and expressed gratitude for the guidance provided by the doctor. The doctor emphasized a collaborative approach, urging the patient to stay in touch if symptoms persisted or worsened.
